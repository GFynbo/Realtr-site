The Inspiration

What's the most stressful thing on your mind after finding a job right after college?

Coming straight out of college, attempting to be an independent adult, but not knowing the next steps to move and find a place to live in a brand new location.

We wanted to simplify this process.

We created a bot that would streamline this difficult time for these seniors so they would be less stressed when kickstarting their exciting careers in a foreign place. Since half our team consists of seniors, we wanted to create a project that was practical and helped us (& other graduating seniors) in this very same situation.
